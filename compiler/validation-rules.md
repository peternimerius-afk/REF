# Validation Rules

**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft

Validation rules check generated or normalized outputs.

## VAL-001 Requirement has one obligation

Every output requirement shall contain one obligation.

---

## VAL-002 Evidence exists

Every requirement shall have at least one evidence item unless policy allows omission.

---

## VAL-003 Evaluation exists

Every requirement shall have an evaluation method.

---

## VAL-004 Score levels are valid

Scoreable requirements shall have objective, mutually exclusive levels.

---

## VAL-005 No prohibited subjective wording

Output requirement text shall not contain prohibited subjective wording.

---

## VAL-006 Word limit

Output requirement text shall comply with policy word limits unless explicitly justified.

---

## VAL-007 Traceability

Every normalized or generated requirement shall reference source or capability origin.
