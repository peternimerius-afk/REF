# ADR 0003: Evidence separation

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-08

## Context

Many existing requirements mix obligations, evidence requests, explanations, and evaluator instructions.

## Decision

REF shall keep requirements, evidence, evaluation, verification, and explanation separate.

## Consequences

- Requirement text becomes shorter.
- Evidence is attached as structured data.
- Evaluation becomes clearer.
- Supplier responses are easier to assess.

## Rationale

Requirements define obligations. Evidence demonstrates compliance. Evaluation determines fulfilment.
