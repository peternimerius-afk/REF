# ADR 0007: AI as assistant, not authority

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-08

## Context

AI can classify, suggest mappings, and propose wording, but procurement decisions must be explainable and repeatable.

## Decision

AI may assist REF processing, but REF rules decide final engineering actions.

## Consequences

- AI output must be traceable to rules.
- Human reviewers can inspect decisions.
- Procurement defensibility improves.
- REF remains useful without any specific AI model.

## Rationale

The explanation for a change shall be rule-based, not "the AI thought it was better."
