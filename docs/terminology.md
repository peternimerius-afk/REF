# Terminology

## Requirement

An objectively evaluable obligation placed on the supplier, solution or service.

## Capability

A functional or non-functional ability that the procured solution or supplier must provide.

## Evidence

Information submitted or referenced to demonstrate compliance.

## Verification

The evaluator activity used to assess evidence.

## Binary requirement

A requirement evaluated as pass/fail or yes/no.

## Scoreable requirement

A requirement evaluated using a graded scoring scale because supplier maturity or quality creates measurable procurement value.

## Knowledge pack

Reusable domain, technology, regulatory or industry knowledge used to guide requirement engineering.

## Policy

A configurable rule set that adjusts REF behavior for a procurement context or organization.

## Gap analysis

Comparison of expected capabilities with capabilities covered by existing requirements.

## Normalization

Transformation of verbose or ambiguous requirement text into atomic, evaluable requirement objects.
