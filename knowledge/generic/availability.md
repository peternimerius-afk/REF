# Availability Capability

## Purpose

Ensure that the service is available at the required level.

## Typical evaluation

Binary for minimum availability. Scoreable only if availability above the minimum creates procurement value.

## Typical evidence

- SLA.
- Availability calculation method.
- Historical availability reports.

## Common mistakes

- Combining availability target, maintenance windows, service credits and evidence in one requirement.
- Using vague terms such as high availability without a measurable target.
