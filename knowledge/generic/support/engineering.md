# Support Engineering Guidance

**Capability ID:** `support`  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft

## Purpose

Ensure that supplier support meets operational needs.

## Engineering strategy

Use binary mandatory requirements for the baseline.

Split the capability into separate requirements when the text contains independent obligations.

Do not embed evidence requests in the requirement statement.

## Baseline requirement pattern

> Supplier support shall be available during agreed service hours.

## Common mistakes

- Using high-quality support without measurable service levels.
- Combining response time, resolution time, escalation, and hours in one requirement.
- Scoring support without clear levels.

## Evaluation guidance

Default evaluation: **binary**

Support maturity is often scoreable where response times, coverage, escalation, language, or critical incident handling differ.
