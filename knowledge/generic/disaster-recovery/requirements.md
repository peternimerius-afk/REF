# Disaster Recovery Requirement Patterns

## Mandatory baseline

> Guaranteed RTO for critical services shall not exceed {rto}.

Evaluation:

> Yes / No

Evidence:

> Disaster recovery plan

## When to split

Split this capability when the requirement also includes:

- Evidence requests
- Reporting obligations
- Testing obligations
- Support obligations
- Separate technical mechanisms
- Contractual remedies

## Scoreable candidate

Scoreable variant: `recovery_maturity`

Use only if higher maturity creates measurable procurement value.
