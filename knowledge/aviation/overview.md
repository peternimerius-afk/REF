# Aviation Knowledge Pack

## Purpose

Provide reusable engineering knowledge for aviation and airport-related procurements.

This pack contains no customer, vendor or airport-specific information.

## Typical capability areas

- Operational messaging.
- Flight information.
- Baggage process integration.
- Resource and stand management.
- Time synchronization.
- Operational resilience.
- Interface monitoring.
- Airport stakeholder integration.

## Engineering guidance

Aviation-specific capabilities should extend the generic NFR catalog.

Industry-specific protocol support is usually binary. Maturity around parsing, validation, monitoring, transformation and operational tooling may be scoreable where it creates measurable procurement value.
