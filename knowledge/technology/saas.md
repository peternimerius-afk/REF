# SaaS Knowledge Pack

## Purpose

Provide engineering guidance for SaaS procurements.

## Typical capabilities

- Availability.
- Tenant isolation.
- Backup and restore.
- Disaster recovery.
- Patch management.
- Identity federation.
- Logging and audit.
- Data export.
- Support model.
- Vulnerability management.

## Evaluation guidance

Most SaaS baseline requirements are binary.

Scoreable requirements may be appropriate for:

- operational resilience;
- API maturity;
- support maturity;
- observability maturity;
- business continuity maturity.
